This is a **demo route** that shows how an itinerary looks — a day-by-day line, distance, budget and a map. The numbers are rounded illustrations.

A compact loop through the best of North Macedonia: the capital Skopje and Matka Canyon, the western mountains of Mavrovo, the UNESCO lakeside of Ohrid with St. Naum, and the southern towns of Bitola and Pelister — then back to Skopje. A car opens up the lakes and mountains; the route also sits next to Albania for a two-country extension.
