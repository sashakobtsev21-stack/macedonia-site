**D Festival**, billed as **North Macedonia's biggest open-air event**, returns to **Lake Dojran** on **26–28 June 2026**. The 2026 line-up is genre-spanning: British post-punk band **IDLES**, techno headliner **Boris Brejcha**, hip-hop legend **DJ Premier** and thrash-metal veterans **Destruction** all top the bill across three days by the lake in the country's far southeast.

## When and where

The festival takes over the shore of **Lake Dojran**, in North Macedonia's **southeastern corner** near the town of Dojran and the regional hub of **Strumica**, on **26, 27 and 28 June 2026**. It is a lakeside, open-air affair — closer to the borders with Greece than to the capital — so it pairs naturally with a trip through the country's quieter southeast rather than the usual Ohrid–Skopje circuit.

## Who is playing

Three nights, four very different headliners. **IDLES** bring raucous live energy, **Boris Brejcha** anchors the electronic side with his high-tech minimal techno, **DJ Premier** represents classic boom-bap hip-hop, and **Destruction** keep the heavier crowd happy. Sets are spread across the festival's stages — the **Main**, **Mosquito** and **Waves** stages — so the programme runs from headline rock and metal to club-night techno after dark.

Coming from abroad? Most visitors fly into **Skopje (SKP)** and drive south.
