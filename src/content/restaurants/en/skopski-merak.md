A long-standing traditional kafana in Debar Maalo, the leafy quarter just west of Skopje's centre that is packed with taverns and cafés. Skopski Merak is one of the most consistently recommended places in the city for Macedonian home cooking — shareable meze, šopska salad, slow-roasted ribs and pastrmajlija (a boat-shaped flatbread topped with cured meat). Most evenings a small acoustic band plays Balkan standards, which is half the reason locals fill the place at weekends. Prices are in Macedonian denar (MKD); a relaxed sit-down dinner sits at the mid range. Confirm hours and book ahead for busy nights.
